# Character Motivations

## King Eldor
- Driven by the desire to honor his father's legacy while forging his own path
- Seeks to prove himself worthy of the crown, especially after the Siege of Eldor
- Wants to maintain peace but is willing to fight when necessary
- Feels the weight of responsibility for his people's safety and prosperity

## King Arion (deceased)
- Was motivated by diplomacy and peaceful resolution
- Believed in the power of negotiation over force
- Died defending his people, which reflects his core values
- His death serves as both inspiration and burden for his son

## Order of the Flame Members
- Dedicated to protecting knowledge and the realm
- Balance between serving the crown and maintaining their independence
- Seek to guide rather than control, offering wisdom when needed

## Shadow Court Operatives
- Motivated by their own mysterious agenda
- May have conflicting loyalties or hidden goals
- Operate in the gray areas between right and wrong
