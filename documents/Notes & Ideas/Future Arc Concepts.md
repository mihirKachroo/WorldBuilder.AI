# Future Arc Concepts

## The Search for Truth
An investigation into King Arion's death reveals unexpected connections and conspiracies that threaten to destabilize the kingdom.

## The Eastern Alliance
A new diplomatic initiative seeks to build bridges with the Eastern Continent, but old wounds and hidden agendas complicate the effort.

## The Velmor Discovery
Explorations of the Ruins of Velmor uncover ancient knowledge that could change everything, but at what cost?

## The Shadow War
The Shadow Court's true agenda comes to light, forcing King Eldor to make difficult choices about allies and enemies.

## The Next Generation
As King Eldor considers his legacy, questions arise about succession and the future of the kingdom.

## The Unification
A grand effort to unite all factions under a common cause, testing the limits of diplomacy and cooperation.
