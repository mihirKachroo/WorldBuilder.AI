# Plot Threads to Resolve

## The Mystery of King Arion's Death
- Was the ambush truly random, or was there a conspiracy?
- Who might have had motive to eliminate the king?
- Could the Shadow Court have been involved?

## The Eastern Continent's True Intentions
- What drove them to launch the Siege of Eldor?
- Are there factions within the continent with different goals?
- Could there be a path to lasting peace?

## The Ruins of Velmor
- What secrets do they hold?
- Why does the Order of the Flame guard them so closely?
- Could they be connected to the kingdom's current challenges?

## The Future of the Royal Guard
- How will they adapt to changing threats?
- What role will they play in the kingdom's future?
- Are there internal conflicts or loyalty issues?

## The Shadow Court's Agenda
- What are their true goals?
- How do they relate to the other factions?
- Will they become allies or enemies?
